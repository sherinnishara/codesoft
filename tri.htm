About the Legend

1.Early Life:

Born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, Virat Kohli showed early promise in cricket and began his journey at a young age.

2.Rise in Cricket:

Kohli rose through the ranks swiftly, representing Delhi in domestic cricket and catching the attention of selectors with his consistent performances.

3.International Debut:

He made his international debut for India in August 2008 in an ODI against Sri Lanka and his Test debut in June 2011 against the West Indies.

4.Batting Maestro:

Kohli is renowned for his exceptional batting skills, known for his impeccable technique, versatility, and ability to adapt to various formats of the game.

5.Records Galore:

He holds numerous records in international cricket, including the fastest century by an Indian in ODIs, the fastest to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in ODIs, and many more.

6.Captaincy:

Kohli has served as the captain of the Indian cricket team across formats, leading the team with aggression, determination, and a winning mindset.

7.IPL Success:

In the Indian Premier League (IPL), he has been a ey player for the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB), showcasing his leadership skills as the team's captain.

8.Off the Field:

Beyond cricket, Kohli is also known for his philanthropic work, fashion ventures, and advocacy for fitness and healthy living.

9.Personal Life:

He is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma, and their relationship often makes headlines in both sports and entertainment media.

10.Legacy:

Virat Kohli's legacy in cricket extends far beyond his statistics; he has inspired countless aspiring cricketers and left an indelible mark on the sport worldwide.
